About me

Christian Noss

Christian Noss
born in 1972 in Gummersbach
first Computer in 1985 – Atari 800XL
Studied Industrial Design at the University of Duisburg-Essen
Student assistant at the University of Essen in the field of ergonomics in design
Working student at ERCO Leuchten GmbH in the Marketing department
Founded Klickmeister GmbH in 2001 an worked with ERCO, Deutsche Bahn, bulthaup and others in the field of design and development.
Certified designer degree with thesis topic E-Dötzchen und Klickmeister. Design strategies for user groups with varying levels of experience» supervised by Prof. Dr. Ralph Bruder and Prof. Claudius Lazzeroni in 2001
Research assistant at the Institute for Ergonomics and Design Research at the University of Duisburg-Essen for 5 years
Professorship for Communication Design in Media Informatics at the University of Applied Sciences primarily teaching design fundamentals, web technologies, and front-end development.
Contribution to the recommendations for the curriculum for bachelor's degree programs in media informatics at the Gesellschaft für Informatik, Media Informatics Section, in 2017
Participation in the development team for the model study program Code & Context
Part of the Cranach Digital Archive research team since 2018
